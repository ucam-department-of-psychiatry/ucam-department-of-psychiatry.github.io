CamCOPS registration

If you are seeing this page it means that CamCOPS patient registration from Android devices isn't working. This could be because:

  • You're not using an Android device.
  • The CamCOPS app isn't installed. Search for CamCOPS on the Google Play Store.
  • The CamCOPS app is installed but the version is too old. Please upgrade to at least version 2.4.22 from the Google Play Store.
  • The version of Android is too old. You may need to enter your registration details manually.

If you are in any doubt, please contact the relevant clinical or study team.
